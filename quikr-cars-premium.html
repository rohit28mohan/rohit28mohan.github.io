About This Project

The premium upsell flow is one of the most crucial phases of monetization for classifieds. Users can upgrade their ad to any of the premium offerings which would boost their position in the overall listing and other benefits.

I had to design it in an iterative manner as we had to roll out a responsive layout quickly and over time we had to add new products to the flow as well.

Project Details

 Quikr
 UX, Visual design, UI
 Aug '16 – Sept '17
 www.quikr.com

 

 The Goal

Improve the UX flow for mobile especially and make it scalable to add more offerings.

 The Result

We saw good adoption my first time users as were able to explain our proposition in a better way. The redesign allowed us to add more premium offering. A responsive code that works both for desktop and mobile.

 The Idea

Do repetitve A/B tests to test the new design and improve it iteratively. Keep in mind that a lot of users are first time individual(private) users who would be selling for the first time.

 The Statistics

By the time we had rolled out the the final post ad flow the increase in overall revenue had been 100%

About the project

The main objective was to improve paid listing adoption in order to increase Sell-side revenues.While we were simultaneously working on improving the Post Ad flow to increase overall Listing Conversion, multiple A/B Tests were carried out in parallel to optimize the Paid Listing funnel.
Additionally, the new Paid Listing UX was expected to handle the increase no. of Paid offerings options that the product was now able to support (3 in total versus 2 at the beginning of the change). The status quo iteration was having a simple comparison grid showcasing the relative advantages/disadvantages of the various paid listing options.
