About This Project

Before rolling out private uploads to all users we planned to improve the overall experience of the flows. Private upload was a premium fature, it allows users to uploads a deck and keep it private it either to himself of share it secretly with other users.

My role was to quickly iterate on the existing product and suggest UI improvements in the existing flows. Also I needed to work on informing users about the private uploads notifictaions (errors, limit reached and other).
